# VALUE Nest Supermarket Analysis (Excel)

## Project Overview
This project provides an in-depth analysis of sales and customer behavior at VALUE Nest Supermarket using Microsoft Excel. The goal was to identify sales trends, customer spending patterns, and branch performance to support business decision-making.

## Objectives
- Analyze sales data across different branches and product categories  
- Identify top-performing branches and products  
- Understand customer demographics and spending behavior  
- Provide actionable insights to improve sales and marketing strategies

## Tools and Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel: Pivot Tables, Charts, Conditional Formatting  
- Descriptive statistics: Total sales, gross margin, quantity and sales distribution  
- Data visualization: Bar charts, line graphs, and dashboards

## Analysis Steps
1. *Data Cleaning:* Verified and cleaned raw sales data to ensure accuracy.  
2. *Sales Summary:* Created pivot tables summarizing sales by branch, product category, and customer demographics.  
3. *Trend Analysis:* Visualized monthly sales trends and identified peak sales periods.  
4. *Customer Insights:* Analyzed spending by gender and product preferences.  
5. *Performance Metrics:* Calculated gross margin and compared performance across branches.

## Key Findings
- November recorded the lowest sales at $445, indicating a need for targeted promotions during this period.  
- Female customers emerged as the top spenders, particularly in the food and beverages category.  
- Gross margin remained consistent across all branches, suggesting uniform pricing strategies.  
- Certain product categories outperformed others, highlighting opportunities for inventory optimization.

## Recommendations
- Implement promotional campaigns in low-sales months like November to boost revenue.  
- Develop targeted marketing strategies focused on female customers, especially in food and beverages.  
- Monitor product category performance regularly to optimize stock levels and reduce waste.  
- Explore opportunities to enhance gross margin through cost control and pricing adjustments.

## Conclusion
This analysis offered valuable insights into VALUE Nest Supermarket's sales dynamics and customer behavior. Leveraging these findings can help drive strategic decisions to improve sales and customer satisfaction.
